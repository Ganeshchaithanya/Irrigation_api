AI design

Two brains.
One farm.

The most important architectural decision — keeping the control brain and the reasoning brain completely separate.

🟢 Control brain

Rule-based.
Deterministic.

Owns all hardware decisions. Runs locally. No API keys. No network dependency. If it says irrigate — it irrigates.
